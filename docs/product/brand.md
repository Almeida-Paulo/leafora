# Leafora Brand

## Core Meaning

Leafora is built from `leaf` plus the classical idea of `phora`: carrying,
bearing, transferring.

The brand meaning:

> Leafora carries ecological proof from the field to the blockchain.

A leaf is the visible unit of a living system. It points to growth, soil, water,
shade, biodiversity and regeneration. The `phora` idea adds movement: evidence,
value and memory being carried from the physical world into a public digital
ledger.

## Brand Position

Leafora is the living ledger for regeneration.

It is not just a donation platform and not a carbon-credit standard at launch.
It is a Web3 platform where ecological projects can be discovered, supported and
verified through public evidence.

## Narrative

The green economy has too many promises and too little proof.

Leafora starts with a simple premise: if a project asks for support, it should
leave a trail. A place, a milestone, a photo, a hash, a timestamp, a wallet, a
public history.

The platform connects capital, community and field evidence so ecological
regeneration can be followed over time.

## Visual System

Primary symbols:

- Leaf: living unit, photosynthesis, regeneration.
- Tree ring: time, memory, accumulated proof.
- Meridian: location, precision, satellite monitoring.
- Ledger line: public record, audit trail, blockchain.

Logo direction:

- A leaf mark whose veins resemble ledger lines, coordinates or block links.
- Avoid generic eco-logo leaves with no technological identity.
- Avoid crypto casino aesthetics.

Tone:

- serious;
- transparent;
- ecological;
- technically credible;
- optimistic without hype.
